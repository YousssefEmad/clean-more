Shipping & Delivery

Delivery

All orders are shipped via trusted local courier partners across Egypt.

Free shipping on orders above EGP 1000.

All shipments come with a tracking number, which will be sent via email or SMS once your order is dispatched.

Standard delivery time is 25 business days, depending on your location.

Returns

Items can be returned within 14 days from the delivery date, provided they are unused and in their original packaging.

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method or as store credit.

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer. Shipping fees from the original order are non-refundable.

Please note that items on sale are final and cannot be returned or exchanged.
